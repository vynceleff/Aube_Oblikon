Pitch / Synopsis

Aube, a young Parisian lab technician, narrowly escapes suicide thanks to Tom, a young scientist obsessed with the origins of the universe. A year earlier, a sudden heart attack following a COVID vaccine left her shaken—and since then, an insidious force seems to creep into her mind, trying to manipulate her will. Shadowy mysterious figures watch her from the edges of her life, while Tom draws closer, compelled by a strange connection born from that fateful incident.

About

This film was conceived as an ambitious independent supernatural thriller, shot in several nice places in Paris, produced with a very small passionate team and small budget, while maintaining full cinematic scope through in-house visual effects, original score, and complete post-production control by the director.
Experienced actors and emerging outstanding ones mixed their talent to give life to multidimensional characters that are at the core of the spectator experience.
